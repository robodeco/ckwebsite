Cambium Portfolio

Formerly the assessment division of the nonprofit American Institute for Research, Cambium Assessment focuses on creating accessible educational testing technologies for students and their families. My duties as a Web Designer II is to support the senior designers in their art direction and site construction, focus on creating semantic and accessible HTML structures and CSS styling, provide assistance in all site maintenance and upkeep, and work with project teams to accomodate client requests. The web technologies/libraries primarily used are javascript/jQuery, php, bootstrap, and some Vue3.

I cannot claim total ownership of any singular website or application, and many of the applications I've worked on are proprietary, but I have provided some site examples that are publicly available.
