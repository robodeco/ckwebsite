Great Design Delights

Hello and welcome to my site! I'm Chris. I've been a graphic designer since 2014 and a web designer since 2019. After attending James Madison University on scholarship and receiving my BA in Graphic Design, I went to work for the big-name grocer Lidl's marketing team as as they made their entrance into the American market. It was a fast-paced environment that demanded precision in all things, but offered an interesting look into the process of a corporate startup.

Over time I realized I couldn't keep designing print leaflets forever; I needed to push myself to learn more. I joined a MERN-stack certification program run by George Washington University, and over the course of 6 months learned how to marry my traditional design skills with emerging web technologies. Desiring to pursue web design further, I shortly left Lidl and went to work for American Institutes for Research; a non-profit organization that focused on building accessible and educational web programs for students and their families.

About one year into my employment with AIR my division was sold to a for-profit corporation called Cambium and was rebranded into Cambium CAI. I'm still there, happily working to make web technologies more accessible for people with disabilities.

I like to follow the mantra, "Great Design Delights." Good design is invisible, bad design is noticed and scorned by everyone, but great design brings delight to all who experience it. This world needs its moments of joy, and I endeavor to contribute what I can!

When I'm not on the job I usually can be found working on the sequel novella to my award-winning breakout novel, The Dance Between. But when I'm not doing that I'm usually gaming, reading, practicing my ukulele, or attending the nearest Renaissance festival.
