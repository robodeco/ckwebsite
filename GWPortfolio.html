GWU Bootcamp Applications

This page contains various projects I worked on during the 6-month period I was enrolled in the George Washington University MERN-stack Coding Bootcamp. Due to the curriculum's rigorous, fast-paced nature I was far less focused on designing pretty applications than I was on learning how to make them functional. I would later become more UI/UX-focused in my role at Cambium.

Click one of the "watch" buttons to view its associated project on the left.
